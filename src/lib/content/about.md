# About Me

Hi — I'm **Trian Damai**, a software engineer originally from a small village in
**East Kalimantan, Borneo**, now based in **Jakarta, Indonesia**.

I started the same way a lot of developers do: curiosity and a patchy internet connection.
My first "projects" were Android app mods — cracking open APKs, tweaking smali, breaking
things to understand what moved. That curiosity turned into a career, and I've since shipped
mobile apps, web platforms, and backend services that real people use every day.

## Currently

- Building mobile apps with **Kotlin**, **Java**, and **Flutter** — mostly Android, occasionally cross-platform
- Shipping web projects with **SvelteKit**, **Nuxt**, and **TypeScript**
- Learning **Rust** — slowly, stubbornly, and enjoying every minute
- Open to new roles where craft and real-world impact both matter

## Background

Growing up in East Borneo, I had to figure most things out on my own — slow internet,
no local dev community, just forums and stubbornness. That self-reliance stuck. I still
prefer understanding things deeply over copying solutions I don't fully understand.

I started with Android mods, moved to building small apps for people around me, went through
college, and eventually found myself writing production backends and shipping products with
real users. Each step was driven by the same thing: wanting to know *how* it actually works.

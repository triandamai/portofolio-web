## What it is

**Tudu** is an Android to-do list app — nothing revolutionary, but that's not the point. I wanted to try out some ideas I had around UI interactions in **Jetpack Compose** and the fastest way to validate them was to just build something real and ship it.

It ended up on the Play Store.

## Why I built it

I'm an Android developer. Ideas come up randomly — a swipe gesture I want to try, a layout pattern I've been thinking about, a way to handle state that I haven't used in production yet. The todo list is the "hello world" of productivity apps, which makes it a perfect sandbox. Low domain complexity, high UI surface area.

Tudu is where I experiment. If something works well here, it usually finds its way into a production app eventually.
